Share The Experience

Life can be lonely when traveling. “The Hitchhikers Guide to Earth” gives you the opportunity to meet fellow travelers. See beautiful and historic attractions. Add them to your wishlist and mark them as visited. Follow and compete with other travelers. Meet new friends or compete with existing ones. The application has something for everyone.

Meet the Team

Arctic Sprint Solutions AS is a fictional Norwegian Company created by students at UiT Norges Arktiske Universitet during the spring of 2024. The company develops software solutions for small to medium sized clients in the tourist industry. Being big fans of Douglas Adams book series “The Hitchhikers Guide to the Galaxy” and passionate about travelling, the group came together when a client contacted them in search for a new platform. The company has its headquarters in Tromso, Norway, but also has branches in northern and western Norway, as well as an international branch in New York City, USA.
